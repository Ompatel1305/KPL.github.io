1. Each player must register for the tournament by paying a fee of Rs 500.

2. After registration, players will be able to participate in the auction.

3. The minimum bid for a team is Rs 2500, and the team will come with 4000 points.

4. With these 4000 points, captain can buy the entire team and take part in the tournament.

5. The tournament is open to all players who have registered for the tournament and have successfully paid the entry fees.

6. The tournament will be an exciting way to showcase cricketing skills and enjoy the game.

7. A captain can draft 4 players before going to auction, each player will cost 700 points.

8. One draft will cost the usage of 700 points out of 4000 points , allotted to the captain for auction.
